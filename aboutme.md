As a data analyst, I am most interested in asking questions and discovering the "why" behind business. I am passionate about uncovering these insights and using them to improve decision-making.

To accomplish this, I utilize R and SQL, along with visualization tools like PowerBI and Tableau, to extract, clean, and analyze data sets. I then gain a complete understanding of the story and share it through stunning visuals. Check out some of my projects [here]().

I have a background in retail, healthcare, and logistics management.

### My Story

I possess a Bachelor's Degree in Exercise Science with a minor in Psychology, having first pursued a career path in physical therapy before switching to analytics. This background provided me with opportunities in clinical settings and provided a strong foundation in scientific method and research that I use as the base for my analysis. During my undergraduate studies, I worked as an operations analyst, helping to streamline inventory processing and develop new automated systems to help the company boost efficiency in the warehouse, getting product on the sales floor faster to increase sales.

During this time, I played a part in developing the company's e-commerce platform and was exposed to CSS and webpage development. It also allowed me to gain insights to different sales channels and how I can optimize productivity across multiple platforms.

Currently, I am continuing my analytical development by earning my Master of Science in Program Evaluation and Data Analytics. I am continuing to add to my skill set and take on new projects and opportunities that expand my portfolio.
